Why I Won't Waste Your Time

In any relationship, the mutual appreciation and respect for each other's time are fundamental. It's the foundation on which trust and affection are built, ensuring that every moment spent together contributes positively to the bond. I am committed to valuing our shared time, focusing on making every interaction meaningful and beneficial for us both. Whether this means we're going travelling to new places together, or sitting at home on the couch watching Haikyuu, I want these moments to have a positive impact on both of us as individuals.

Honest communication is the cornerstone of any successful relationship. It ensures that both partners understand each other’s feelings, expectations, and life goals, thus preventing potential conflicts and misunderstandings. When it comes to us, I am finding more and more that it's easy to talk to you when I am feeling down about something, and I want to be that for you too. I believe that my earnest and sincere desire to do the same for you, I would not waste your time and would be a good partner for honest communication.

Engaging in shared interests that both partners enjoy significantly enhances the quality of the relationship. It allows us to share in each other’s joys and discover new ones together, making every quality moment spent together both enjoyable and memorable. While we do not have much in the ways of common interests (yet), we make up for it by trying new things and spending quality time with one another. It's from these experiences that we are learning more about ourselves and our likes/dislikes, and I for one am finding out more and more about the things I enjoy thanks to exploring them with you.

Respecting each other’s individuality and personal goals is essential for a healthy relationship. It means supporting each other’s ambitions and growth without feeling threatened or neglected. Should you choose to date me, I will always be here to support your growth as an individual, and be there to give you the push you need. You may not need me to be there, but even the toughest of people can appreciate a helping hand where originally there was none.

I am committed to maintaining honest communication, sharing quality time through newly-found mutual interests, and respecting our individual paths to personal growth. This approach not only ensures that we do not waste each other's time but also enriches our experiences together. By focusing on these principles, I reaffirm my dedication to building a fulfilling and enduring relationship, grounded in mutual respect and shared adventures. Together, we can ensure that every moment that we will spend together is valued and meaningful.
